ABOUT ME - BRAND

Why did you create your brand?

After starting with branding design, I found my old brand was outdated and candidly repugnant. So, I set out to create a new image for myself, I needed something clean and attractive when I came up with the stylized "M" and added my, now signature, gradient I knew I had found exactly what I'd coveted. Then my brand was born, it suffered some variations like the photography watermark and the mark (added a circumference around it) but remained uncomplicated and polished.
